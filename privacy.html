Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 25, 2026

At ama, we believe your personal business should stay exactly that—personal. We’ve built our tech to ensure you can analyze the vibe without compromising your data.

1. Zero-Storage Policy

Your chats are your own. We do not store your messages, screenshots, or uploaded text on our servers. Analysis is performed through secure, transient processing that wipes data the moment your "Vibe Check" is complete.

2. On-Device Intelligence

Wherever possible, ama uses Apple's Neural Engine to process "Red Flag Detection" directly on your iPhone. This means the data never even leaves your device.

3. Analytics & Improvement

We collect anonymous metadata (like crash reports or feature usage) to make the app better. This data is never linked to your identity or your psychological insights.

4. Contact Us

Questions about your data? Our support team is ready to clear things up. Reach out at support@ama-app.com.
