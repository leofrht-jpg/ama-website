Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2, 2026

At ama - advanced mood analyzer, we believe your personal business should stay exactly that—personal. We’ve built our tech to ensure you can analyze the vibe without compromising your privacy.

1. Chat Analysis & Content

To provide relationship insights, we process the text messages (User Content) you voluntarily upload. This analysis is performed securely using third-party AI partners. This processing is transient: your raw chat logs are analyzed to generate the report and are not permanently stored on our servers or used to train public AI models.

2. No Account Required

We do not collect your email address or require you to create an account. Instead, we use anonymous Device Identifiers to manage your Pro subscription and settings. This ensures your usage remains private and unlinked to your personal contact information.

3. Subscriptions & Purchases

If you purchase a Pro subscription, we process your Purchase History via RevenueCat and Apple to validate your subscription status. We do not have access to your direct credit card or banking information.

4. Analytics & Diagnostics

We use tools like Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to collect usage data (such as button taps, screen views, and crash logs). While this data is linked to your anonymous Device ID to help us troubleshoot issues and improve the app, we do not share this data with third-party data brokers or use it for advertising tracking.

5. Contact Us

Questions about your data? Our support team is ready to clear things up. Reach out at support@advancedmoodanalyzer.com.
